Himalayas


The Himalayas in India stretch across a vast area in the northern part of the country, primarily encompassing the states of Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Sikkim, and Arunachal Pradesh. They also extend into the hill regions of Assam and West Bengal.
